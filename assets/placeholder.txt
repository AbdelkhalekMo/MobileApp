This directory should contain the following images:

1. logo.png - Your app logo
2. favicon.ico - Website favicon
3. hero-app.png - Main app screenshot for the hero section
4. app-screen1.png and app-screen2.png - Floating app screenshots
5. step1.png, step2.png, step3.png - Images for the "How It Works" section
6. screen1.png through screen5.png - App screenshots for the carousel
7. google-play.png and app-store.png - Store badges
8. phone-frame.png - Phone frame to overlay on app screenshots
9. phone-mockup1.png, phone-mockup2.png, phone-mockup3.png - Phone mockups with app screens

You can use images from your own app or create placeholder images using tools like:
- Figma
- Adobe XD
- Sketch
- Canva
- Placeit.net

For quick placeholders, you can also use services like:
- https://placeholder.com/
- https://placehold.co/
- https://picsum.photos/

Example usage for placeholder:
https://placehold.co/300x600?text=App+Screenshot 

For phone mockups, you can use:
- https://mockuphone.com/
- https://smartmockups.com/
- https://www.freepik.com/free-photos-vectors/phone-mockup
